| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-03 | Spoofing | ProtectedRoute | medium | mitigate | Loading state check (authLoading OR empresaLoading) prevents premature redirect before session resolves — per Pitfall 6 |
| T-02-04 | Tampering | empresa.service.ts upsert | medium | mitigate | onConflict: 'user_id' + UNIQUE constraint (migration 0002) prevents duplicate rows; RLS policy enforces user_id = auth.uid() |
| T-02-05 | Information Disclosure | EmpresaProvider | low | accept | getForCurrentUser() only returns the calling user's row — RLS enforced at DB level |
| T-02-06 | Elevation of Privilege | OnboardingGuard | medium | mitigate | Unauthenticated users hitting /onboarding are redirected to /welcome — no empresa_mei data exposed |
</threat_model>
